# Screenshots Guide - Ilm o Kutub System

This guide outlines the location, capture specifications, and suggested captions for screenshots required for the project documentation.

---

## 1. Capture Guidelines
* **Resolution**: Capture screenshots at standard high resolution (minimum 1280x720).
* **Theme**: Use the default professional charcoal/blue theme configurations.
* **Format**: Save screenshots as PNG files under `docs/screenshots/` in the repository.

---
